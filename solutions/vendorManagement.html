        Your Comprehensive Vendor Management CSR Solution

        All stakeholders united on one platform, unlocking limitless possibilities

        The TraceMe all-in-one compliance & sustainability management solution facilitates seamless data exchange, communication and collaborative efforts among you and your value chain partners, paving the way for more impactful CSR achievements.

        Unlock Comprehensive CSR Excellence with TraceMe

        With traceMe, you gain access to a unified platform that empowers you to seamlessly visualize, verify and communicate your entire value chain. This comprehensive tool allows you to master your CSR initiatives, promote cross-company collaboration and establish a foundation of trust with your customers.


        Say goodbye to the complexity of managing multiple systems and processes. With traceMe, you have the simplicity of a single platform that streamlines your operations, fosters CSR excellence, encourages collaboration across organizations and reinforces trust throughout your supply chain.

        Key features

        RISK ANALYSIS & MANAGEMENT

        Comprehend your risk factors, prioritize and act decisively

        Streamline your workflow with automated risk analysis and real-time insights from current global industry risk data and indices.

        Efficiently prioritize mitigation strategies using automated, detailed risk profiles for every company in your network.

        Ensure compliance with all local and EU-related supply chain due diligence regulations.

        SUPPLIER MANAGEMENT & ASSESSMENTS

        Gather CSR data from stakeholders, assess it, and collectively manage it.

        Effortlessly capture pertinent certificates, documents and sustainability information through instant digitalized onboarding of new suppliers.

        Efficiently manage and request current supplier data, including production, compliance and sustainability information, consolidating it into a single point of truth.

        Simple setup and intuitive visualization allow for immediate assessments of a supplier's alignment with your requirements.

        Additional features for even more valuable insights.

        Product and Material Database

        Efficiently gather and track all pertinent supplier, product and material information in a single centralized location.

        SC mapping & visualization

        Explore and visualize your supply chains to maintain a clear overview of the origins of your products and materials.

        Scalability

        Streamline audit coordination with your suppliers and facilitate efficient communication and progress tracking for Corrective Action Plans (CAPs).

        CSR Dashboard

        Stay informed about sustainability within your supply chains and keep track of the latest compliance updates.

        Customer communication

        Effectively convey your sustainability initiatives at the point of sale to gain consumer trust and foster loyalty.

        An all-in-one CSR platform fostering inter-company collaboration for responsible transformation
