        YOUR TRACEABILITY SOLUTION

        Expandable Traceability. All Materials. Every Supply Chain.

        Trace and authenticate your supply chains to the source of raw materials, enhance operational transparency, document the chain of custody and meet your clients' disclosure demands.

        Revisit your journey for the right motives

        Tracing is pivotal in establishing accountability within supply chains determining the origins, makers and processes involved in products and materials. Legal mandates such as the German Supply Chain Due Diligence Law and the Uyghur Forced Labor Prevention Act have elevated the significance of tracing and risk management as essential aspects of conducting business. The time to take action is now.


        With traceMe, you can efficiently organize and optimize your supply chain traceability, securing transparent data to validate product and material provenance and readily share it with your brand and retail partners.

        Select the traceability solution that aligns perfectly with your requirements

        FIBRE-FORWARD TRACING

        Establishing Fiber-Forward Provenance from the Source

        Fiber-forward tracing begins with identifying upstream suppliers and follows the material's journey from farm to final garment.

        This form of tracing facilitates extensive internal tracking and digital connectivity throughout production steps, enhancing visibility, particularly with upstream suppliers.

        All supply chain collaborators, spanning from farmers to fabric finishers, can unite on a single platform to collect, share, and report pertinent data, fostering collaborative efforts towards a transparent and sustainable value chain.

        PRODUCT-BACKWARD TRACING

        Multi-Tier Visibility Anchored in Orders and Verified Proof Points

        Product backward tracing initiates at the retailer, enabling the collection of order-based chain-of-custody data as it flows top-down through the supply chain.

        Product backward traceability empowers partners to input a purchase order number and access all essential documentation required for risk management and compliance with global reporting standards.

        Product backward tracing offers full scalability, providing comprehensive support for large businesses as they address their reporting and disclosure challenges.

        Leading the way in traceability with traceMe

        Tailored to meet your needs

        We recognize that every supply chain is unique. Our approach is to collaborate with you, understand your tracing requirements and align them with your production and business strategy.

        Alignment of Compliance Strategy

        All tracing solutions within retraced seamlessly integrate with our comprehensive compliance management platform, ensuring that all data from all parties is digitally synchronized within a single system.

        Empower Your Upstream Suppliers

        Tracing delivers comprehensive supply chain visibility, allowing data input from all levels, including traditionally hard-to-reach upstream suppliers such as farmers, ginners and spinners.

        Operational Excellence

        Real-time advanced traceability and chain-of-custody documentation empower you to bridge gaps in your supply chain network and consolidate supply chains at deeper tiers and production processes.

        Reporting

        Collected compliance information can be exported from the platform and shared with business partners to meet reporting and regulatory obligations.
