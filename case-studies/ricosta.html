With Ricosta we are strengthening long-standing tradition by digitizing supply chains

Full compliance readiness

THE CHALLENGE

Ricosta was looking to develop and optimize their processes with the help of a tool that is easy to use and can help them with their compliance management.


As an industry leader in sustainability, a continued development and monitoring of internal processes is of high importance. But digitalization often means dealing with complex tools that require staff to be adequately trained.

OUR SOLUTION

With traceme, Ricosta was able to seamlessly implement the traceme software into the daily business routine through an easily understandable on-boarding process and the long-term support and guidance by the traceme customer excellence team.


The traceme platform enables Ricosta to gain a clear overview of all suppliers, risk factors, documents and certificates to always remain up-to-date and ready for all future compliance requirements.

“As a traditional German family business, Ricosta is demonstrating through their use of the traceme platform that digitalization and compliance management doesn’t have to be difficult.”
