        Tackle Your CSR Goals from All Angles, All on One Platform

        Enhancement begins with transparency

        Effective compliance and CSR management initiatives commence with the practical transparency of your fashion supply chains. Given the intricate nature of these supply networks, identifying risks and achieving sustainability goals can prove challenging, particularly when comprehensive visibility across your supply chains is lacking.


        TraceMe simplifies complex supply chain data management. In a world where stakeholders are dispersed and often disconnected, our platform automates data collection, saving you valuable time. Say goodbye to chasing down documents and spreadsheets. With traceMe, gain the visibility needed to meet your goals effectively.

        Source: KMPG Report Dec 2021

        Our CSR software empowers you to make a meaningful impact

        Begin by evaluating your risks and assessing your position on your sustainability journey. Establish connections with your critical material and fabric suppliers to access pertinent data promptly. Utilize traceMe's vendor management solution to track your supplier's sustainability standards and receive real-time insights into supply chain risk factors.

        Effortlessly navigate your transparency and compliance management initiatives with precision. Handle and assess sustainability at both product and supplier levels seamlessly. Safeguard against potential risks with the requisite proof and documentation. With traceMe's CSR software, you can automate the collection of critical supply chain data insights, centralizing them in one accessible location, empowering you to take swift action to attain your objectives.

        Overcome today's and tomorrow's compliance hurdles as a united front. Maintain a comprehensive, up-to-the-minute view of your sustainability efforts. Effectively communicate, share, and report your progress with pertinent stakeholders. At traceMe, we collaborate with you to bolster your CSR strategy and facilitate supplier cooperation in the joint management of sustainable supply chains.
